Online Payment : Shoplz Pay, Billplz, MOLPay, 2c2p

For your convenience, receive payments from your customers instantly online, and speed up the closing processes.

To enable this method, you need to have an existing account of payment gateway for either Billplz account, MOLPay or 2c2p.

If you don’t, you can opt to use Shoplz Pay.

Online Payment Advantages

If your business are more on online transaction, we recommend Online Payment as customer’s method of payment. It’s easy and save customer’s and your valuable time.

Easy For Customers = More Sales

There is a small transaction fee of RM0.50-RM1.50 if you use payment gateway service for Online Payment. But you can choose to include the fee inside shipping fee (Not your product price). Shoplz Pay transaction fee is cheaper compare to all 3 other payment gateways.
