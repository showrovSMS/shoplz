Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

These are the questions our customers ask most frequently

  • What is Shoplz?
  • Shoplz is Software as a Service (SaaS) for businesses to grow their sell by expanding with WhatApp and Line forms is an easy way to receive payments for goods and services. We want to make shopping more convenient where customers needn’t leave their chat window to finalize a purchase, the possibilities become endless.
  • How is Shoplz different from other e-commerce platforms?
  • Shoplz is a lightweight website and easy setup your store just in a few minutes. Plus, Shoplz has a free plan.
  • Are there setup or transaction fees?
  • No. There are no setup or transaction fees with any of our plans.
  • Do you charge bandwidth or storage fees?
  • No. All Shoplz plans include unlimited bandwidth and storage at no extra charge.
  • Can I use my own domain name or an existing site?
  • Absolutely. Your Shoplz store can be added to an existing website residing on your own domain name.
  • What currency will I be billed in?
  • All plans are billed in MYR ringgit.
  • What payment options do you support?
  • Shoplz supports over 11 payment providers globally, including PayPal and Stripe, so there’s a good chance we support your preferred option.
  • Do I have to pay for it?
  • Well, no! After the 14 days free trial, you need to decide if you wish to use our free plan or subscribe to one of our premium plans. We’re committed to bringing you the most powerful tools to manage your customer while keeping our product extremely customizable and easy to use. You can do it all and much more with mobile web that is highly customizable for your business. Totally worth it, right?
  • A free trial? I don’t get it…
  • Ok, so! Everyone gets started with the free 14-day trial, which includes everything Shoplz has to offer, so you can truly experience the product with no limitations. By the end of the free trial, you can choose whether you subscribe to a premium plan or use the free plan with up to 10 products. Bear in mind that the free plan doesn’t include everything you experienced during your free trial, which means some capabilities will be lost.
  • How does the free trial work?
  • For 14 days, you can use Shoplz’s full capabilities, completely free (you’ll experience the Expert plan). No credit card needed, no strings attached. During those 14 days, our Customer Success Team will help you understand if Shoplz is the right solution for your business.
  • What happens when my free trial is over?
  • After a free 14 day trial, you need to decide if you will continue using Shoplz. If you do, you need to subscribe to one of our premium plans or use the free plan. We try to make this decision as easy as possible, that’s why we offer a monthly subscription that can be canceled at any time and an annual subscription with a 30-day refund policy. If you don’t want to subscribe, all your information will stay intangible until you decide to subscribe or delete the account upon request.
  • What are the benefits of the Expert plan?
  • The Expert plan is for companies that want to automate procedures, add more security layers, and use Shoplz’s most advanced system capabilities, for up to 10 users with unlimited Whatsapp and Line forms. If you have more questions about our plans, please feel free to reach out to our Customer Success Team!
  • What’s the benefit of the Advanced Plan over Basic plan?
  • We named it as it really is – a more advanced offering. The Advanced plan provides more automation, more modifications, more customization options, and, well, more advanced use of the platform.Which one should you choose? If you’re new to Shoplz, we suggest you begin with our Basic plan. As your use of Shoplz gets deeper, you can upgrade to the Advanced or Expert plan.
  • Which plan is right for me?
  • That’s a good question, but not a tough one to answer. If you have less than 4 users, we suggest starting with the Basic plan and going from there. Or, reach out to our Customer Success Team – we’ll be happy to help you figure it out.
  • How does your pricing work?
  • We have 3 main plans that include all of our main capabilities for up to 10 users. Yes, you read correctly. We also have an Advance plan for companies with up to 9 users. Contact our Customer Success Team for more information about the Advanced plan. Small businesses that don’t want to subscribe to an Advance plan can use the Basic plan with up to 4 users!
  • After purchase, can I change plans?
  • Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. For annual subscriptions only, we have a 30-day refund policy.
  • Do you offer a fully branded mobile app?
  • No, we don’t. But worry not, we will soon offer this fully branded mobile app for IOS and Android devices.
